🔒 Why Mesh is Most Private & Secure

▼

🚫 Zero Servers

Your messages never touch our servers or anyone else's. Direct peer-to-peer connections mean only you and your contacts see your data.

🔐 End-to-End Encryption

Every message is cryptographically signed with ECDSA-P256. Even if intercepted, your communications remain mathematically unbreakable.

🌐 Mesh Network

Messages relay through connected peers, creating a resilient network that can't be shut down by any central authority.

👤 True Anonymity

No phone numbers, email addresses, or personal information required. Generate new identities instantly with zero trace.

🏠 Runs Locally

Everything happens in your browser using WebRTC. No apps to install, no permissions to grant, no data to mine.

🔄 Self-Healing

If one peer goes offline, messages automatically route through others. The network adapts and survives without central coordination.

🎯 The Bottom Line

Mesh doesn't just protect your privacy - it makes surveillance architecturally impossible. Unlike Signal, WhatsApp, or Telegram that still route through servers (creating chokepoints for control), Mesh creates a truly distributed network where your conversations exist only between you and your contacts. No company can sell your data, no government can demand backdoors, and no hacker can breach a server that doesn't exist.
